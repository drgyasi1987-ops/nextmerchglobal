Our Story

How NextMerch Started

NextMerch Global Ltd. was founded by husband-and-wife team Dr. Kwasi Boakye-Gyasi and Mrs. Ivy Boakye-Gyasi after years of firsthand experience navigating international procurement, supplier relationships, and product development.


Recognizing the need for a procurement company built on integrity, transparency, and professionalism, they established NextMerch to bridge the gap between trusted manufacturers and organizations seeking dependable supply partners.


Today, the company combines more than twenty years of collective experience in sourcing, procurement, business development, product design, and supply chain management.
